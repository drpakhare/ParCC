# ParCC: Parameter Converter and Calculator for HTA

A decision support tool for Health Technology Assessment (HTA). Bridges
the gap between clinical evidence and economic models by automating
complex parameter estimations including rate-probability conversions,
hazard ratio-based probability conversion, survival extrapolation, PSA
distribution fitting, and value-based pricing analysis.
